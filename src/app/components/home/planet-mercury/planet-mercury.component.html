MERCURY

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System. Its orbit around the sun takes 87.97 days, the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System. It is named after the Roman deity Mercury, the messenger of the gods.

Mercury's surface is a rugged landscape covered in tens of thousands of impact craters, showcasing its ancient history. From the surface, the Sun appears more than three times larger than from Earth, casting intense sunlight up to 11 times brighter.
